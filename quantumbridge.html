THE PROBLEM

A Massive Market Locked Out of the AI Revolution

Highly regulated industries—healthcare systems, banks, insurance companies, aerospace firms—are sitting on enormously valuable data. They know AI could transform their operations: automating clinical documentation, detecting fraud, accelerating research.

But they're stuck. Sending patient records or financial data to OpenAI or Anthropic's cloud creates compliance nightmares. HIPAA violations can cost millions. GDPR breaches trigger percentage-of-revenue fines.

So these organizations either avoid AI entirely or build expensive, inferior in-house solutions.

The result: enterprise AI budgets frozen by compliance concerns.

HOW IT WORKS

Five Steps. Complete Protection.

01
INGRESS
Data Intake
Data comes in from the enterprise—patient notes, financial records, claims data. Healthcare-native connectors (HL7/FHIR) reduce integration friction.
02
DE-IDENTIFY
Strip Sensitive Data
QuantumBridge strips out names, SSNs, medical record numbers, and all protected information using advanced de-identification.
03
ROUTE
Policy-Driven Routing
Sanitized data goes to the AI model. Policy engine enforces rules about what data can go where, which models for which purposes.
04
PROCESS
AI Response
Response comes back from OpenAI, Anthropic, or other providers. QuantumBridge re-attaches identifying information only where permitted.
05
AUDIT
Immutable Logging
Everything is logged for audit trails, with retention policies matching regulatory requirements. Satisfies regulators and compliance teams.
∞
RESULT
Compliance Peace
The enterprise gets AI capabilities. The AI vendor never sees protected data. Compliance teams sleep at night.
